Inclusive Development of Children and Youngsters with Disabilities

Child Development

Physiotherapy

Children with hemiparesis are given regular massage and exercise to improve the movements of fingers, hand and legs. Mothers/ caretakers are given practice to continue the regular massage and make the child do the exercise for six months.

Nutrition

Fulfillment of basic needs keeps the children healthy, free from symptomatic illness and cheerful. Children who are weak were provided nutrients. Food and snacks during the residential program, refreshments in the monthly meetings and periodical gatherings kept the children happy and active.

Medication

Chetana always takes initiative to attend to the felt needs of all the children and youngsters who are under our care.


Assistive Device

We provided necessary assistive devices to improve the mobility of the children and to meet their daily needs. Two of them were given wheelchairs and one was given a commode chair.

Education

The Children and youngsters under our care are all continuing their education at various levels. We equip them with coaching fee, School fee, study materials, Uniform etc.

Social inclusion

We are keen on family visits whereby we express our interest in any decisions concerned with the education and developmental activities of the child. During these visits we ensure about the academics, participation of the child in household activities and social nature of the child.

Enabling Environment

Summer program

With the objective of overall development of the children we organize day programs for the children during their summer school holidays. During these day programs children are given different classes on their school learning subjects, inspirational classes, games and entertainment. The physical needs like food, snacks, and massage, exercise and entertainment programs are part of the summer Day program.

Exposure

A day of exposure is part of the Summer Day programs. We go to the near-by places like parks, gardens or dams, and spend the day with a lot of games and entertainment. These programs remain memorable to all the children.



Foundation Day

The academic year starts with foundation day celebration of Chetana. We make this day a reason to gather all our children, youngsters and their caretakers. We organize an orientation program on that day to analyze the school education of the previous year, make needed changes in the present year. We also conduct games for adults and youngsters.

Children’s Day

Children’s Day is celebrated on 14th November. Along with children of the Chirag we also include other children from the slum. We have a small input session with the importance of the day, then games and food. The whole day is spent together having various activities.

Disability Day

Disability day is celebrated on 2nd December. Mainly the women folk are the beneficiaries of the program. We mainly focus on various health issues on this day.

Christmas and New Year Gathering

We celebrate Christmas and New year with all the children. We try to make the day joyful and tell them why we care for them. All the children and caretakers of some come on that day. We wish them good health and success in the New Year.

Rehabilitation
